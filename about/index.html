About

Twenty years in this industry, and I’ve watched the scenery change many times. Languages come and go, tools rise and die, trends recycled with new names. But under all that noise, it’s still about solving problems cleanly and building systems that don’t collapse under pressure.

I’ve written code for everything from finance and payments to property tech and SaaS platforms. I’ve led teams, fixed broken pipelines, spun up infrastructure in the cloud and learned what actually matters: reliability, and good judgement. These days, I’m focused on refining those skills, not chasing hype.

In 2025, I founded Synedrus. It’s my way of applying hard-earned experience helping teams stabilise, secure, and ship faster.

I care about clean engineering, thoughtful automation, and the craft of building things that endure.

This site is where I share what I’ve learned (and unlearned) about software and systems.

Cloud architect and DevSecOps specialist with two decades in the trenches. Founder of @synedruscomau.
